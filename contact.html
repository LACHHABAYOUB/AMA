The Moroccan Association Annobough (AMA) was created in June 18, 2013. It is an association, which arouses the interest of the creativity of the talents with the general publicand encourage creative and innovative activities in educational institutions, and identify creative talents to support and promote the development of science and technology so as to serve the economic structure and social development and contribute to build the country in order to become a developed country.



JOIN US, LEAD A CHANGE

ABOUT US

Housed at the Bouchentouf Youth Center in Casablanca, its main mission is to encourage creativity among young people enrolled in educational institutions in the Region, including a regular process of identifying better creative talents and to accompany them in the promotion and development of their innovations; which contributes to advancing science and technology in Morocco.
