"AIN"Arab Network for Innovation

Is a registered charity in the United Kingdom under number 1155087. Our vision in Ain is to spread the culture of innovation and creativity in the Arab homeland by bridging the gap between academic training and work by organizing many activities : annual conference, competition.

Over the past six years, the congress has been held in Jordan, the United Arab Emirates and Sudan under the patronage of Their Majesties and Excellencies, Queen Rania Al Abdullah who supported us at the 2015 AINAC Congress in Jordan. During these years, we celebrated hundreds of innovations from students from the Arab world, and we followed tens of thousands of people on our various pages on social media platforms, and hosted many Arab and international dignitaries as well as a handful of Arab youtubers and other Excellencies, as well as entrepreneurs in the Arab world beyond fifty personal represents the World Success story, and benefited from the workshops of nearly a thousand participants from various Arab countries and several countries of the world.



The main activities of the Congress

1-The challenges of companies

Business challenges aim to connect businesses to the challenge with young people and by providing a real problem facing the company as part of its work and the opportunity for young people, recent graduates and students of the past year undergraduate and interested and / or specialists in the field of challenge category, to participate in launch solutions And creative ideas in the procedures determined by the company and in cooperation with the network team. The winning team is the one that offers the most convincing and viable solutions to present at the annual "AINAC" conference The challenges are: engineering, industry, health, environment, renewable energy and entrepreneurship. The winner receives training covers all expenses of more than three months in the host company with the possibility of obtaining a permanent job in case of proven prices or material to be determined by the company. Contributed to us in the challenges of previous years, large companies such as Aramex and global manufacturing and core of pharmaceutical research in Egypt, in addition to the challenge of the Ministry of Development in Sudan.

2-Exhibition of opportunities "AINCOM"

Is an interactive platform during the two days of the Congress, where large Arab and foreign companies, organizations and initiatives from around the world offer various opportunities: jobs, training, business advice, scholarships, training, creative and innovative games, technological products and heritage.

3-the workshops

Set of workshops with certificates accredited by suppliers and aim to provide participants with the skills and information that help them in the preparation and knowledge of skills and requirements necessary for graduation and entry into life active. AINAC workshops are offered in many areas such as self-development, science and engineering, technology, medicine and the environment, presented by academics and scientists from various scientific and educational institutions and centers. universities around the world.

Inspiring 4-Words and Creative Presentations of Inspirations on the Arab World at the Opening and Closing Ceremonies

At opening and closing ceremonies, we usually welcome inspiring people who have provided their communities with innovations that have facilitated human life and developed society as a success in the overall theme of the Congress. We also welcome some of the young innovators and creators in the field of recreational activities, be they cultural shows, performances or artistic performances that serve the theme of the General Congress. Over the last five years we have hosted international personalities such as Prof. Dr. Talal Abu-Ghazaleh, the owner of Talal Abu-Ghazaleh International Group, and D.Fawaz Bitar Regional Director of PB, who was awarded first prize in the Science Stars and M.aman Najib, as well as a number of Arab youtubers and other Excellencies and leaders in the Arab world.



Student Communities

These are student societies representing the Arab Network for Innovation in universities in the Arab world, managed by prominent students in the same universities, who aim to spread the culture of innovation at the university and educational levels.

These student communities are spread across Lebanon, Palestine, Jordan, Egypt and Sudan.
